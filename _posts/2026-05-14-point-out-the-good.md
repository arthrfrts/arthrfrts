Topher Kearby, via [swissmiss](https://www.swiss-miss.com/2026/05/point-out-the-good.html):

> point out the good  
> when you see it.
>
> in life,  
> in others,  
> in yourself.
>
> because the world  
> needs to remember  
> what kindness and  
> love look like.
